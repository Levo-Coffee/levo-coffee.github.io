About Levo Coffee

Levo Coffee is a boutique consulting firm specializing in coffee sourcing and quality control at origin. We bridge the gap between exceptional coffee producers and discerning buyers, ensuring that every cup tells a story of quality, sustainability, and craftsmanship.

Our team brings decades of combined experience in coffee production, cupping, and international trade. We work directly with farmers and cooperatives, conducting rigorous quality assessments and facilitating transparent, fair-trade relationships.
